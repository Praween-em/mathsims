Trigonometric Ratios of 45° — Isosceles Right Triangle

Step-by-Step Proof

STEP 1
Draw isosceles right △ABC
with ∠B = 90°, ∠A = ∠C = 45°
STEP 2
Since ∠A = ∠C, the sides opposite are equal:
AB = BC = a
STEP 3
By Pythagoras theorem:
AC² = AB² + BC² = a² + a² = 2a²
AC = a√2
STEP 4
sin 45° = opp/hyp = a / a√2 = 1/√2
cos 45° = adj/hyp = a / a√2 = 1/√2
tan 45° = opp/adj = a / a = 1
